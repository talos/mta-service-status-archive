Substitute buses will be provided for customers traveling to Central Islip and Ronkonkoma. Please plan for 31 minutes extra travel time. &lt;br&gt;·       The 3:07 PM train from Penn Station to Ronkonkoma (usual arrival time 4:25 PM) will depart Penn two minutes later at 3:09 PM and arrive Ronkonkoma at 4:30 PM. &lt;br&gt;·       The 7:11 PM peak train from Penn Station to Ronkonkoma (usual arrival time 8:33 PM) will depart Penn six minutes later at 7:17 PM and arrive Ronkonkoma at 8:43 PM. &lt;br&gt;&lt;br&gt;&lt;b&gt;Pickup a copy of the Ronkonkoma Branch weekday timetable dated April 30, 2018 or link here: &lt;/b&gt; &lt;a href="http://web.mta.info/lirr/Timetable/4-30%20to%205-18-2018%20-%20KO%20-%20ML2T%20Cutovers%20-%20GO%20304.pdf"&gt;RonkonkomaBranch&lt;/a&gt;&lt;br&gt;&lt;br&gt;&lt;b&gt;For more information&lt;/b&gt;, contact the LIRR's Customer Service Center by calling 511 or 718-217-LIRR (718-217-5477).
