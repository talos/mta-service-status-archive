                  &lt;h3&gt;&lt;span&gt;Monday, April 30 - Weekday Schedule Changes Begin on Ronkonkoma Branch&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/h3&gt;&lt;h3&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/h3&gt;&lt;span&gt;As the Double Track project moves into its final phases, weekday schedule changes are needed in order to remove the existing siding at Central Islip and install the second through-running track. Although most changes impact off-peak service, several rush hour trains are affected, starting Monday, April 30 and continuing through the end of summer.  Changes include:&lt;br&gt;&lt;br&gt;&lt;u&gt;&lt;i&gt;Westbound:&lt;/i&gt;&lt;/u&gt;&lt;br&gt; ·       &lt;b&gt;The 7:04AM peak train from Ronkonkoma to Penn Station will originate at Brentwood. Substitute buses will be provided for customers traveling to Central Islip and Ronkonkoma. Please plan for 31 minutes extra travel time. &lt;br&gt;·       The 3:07 PM train from Penn Station to Ronkonkoma (usual arrival time 4:25 PM) will depart Penn two minutes later at 3:09 PM and arrive Ronkonkoma at 4:30 PM. &lt;br&gt;·       The 7:11 PM peak train from Penn Station to Ronkonkoma (usual arrival time 8:33 PM) will depart Penn six minutes later at 7:17 PM and arrive Ronkonkoma at 8:43 PM. &lt;br&gt;&lt;br&gt;&lt;b&gt;Pickup a copy of the Ronkonkoma Branch weekday timetable dated April 30, 2018 or link here: &lt;/b&gt; &lt;a href="http://web.mta.info/lirr/Timetable/4-30%20to%205-18-2018%20-%20KO%20-%20ML2T%20Cutovers%20-%20GO%20304.pdf"&gt;RonkonkomaBranch&lt;/a&gt;&lt;br&gt;&lt;br&gt;&lt;b&gt;For more information&lt;/b&gt;, contact the LIRR's Customer Service Center by calling 511 or 718-217-LIRR (718-217-5477).
