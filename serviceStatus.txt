&lt;/font&gt;&lt;p style="margin: 0in 0in 0pt;"&gt;&lt;b&gt;&lt;u&gt;&lt;font size="3"&gt;&lt;font face="Calibri"&gt;Buses Replace Trains between Patchogue &amp; Montauk
Nov. 10-1&lt;span style="color: rgb(31, 73, 125);"&gt;2&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/u&gt;&lt;/b&gt;&lt;/p&gt;&lt;font face="Times New Roman" size="3"&gt;

&lt;/font&gt;&lt;p style="background: white;"&gt;&lt;span style='font-family: "Helvetica",sans-serif; font-size: 10pt;'&gt;The
Long Island Rail Road is modernizing the Montauk Branch, extending the high
capacity electric signal system in place throughout most of the railroad 45
miles eastward from Speonk all the way to Montauk. The move will mean even
greater train safety and reliability, will enable future service upgrades, and
paves the way for the implementation of Positive Train Control.&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/p&gt;&lt;font face="Times New Roman" size="3"&gt;

&lt;/font&gt;&lt;p style="background: white;"&gt;&lt;span style='font-family: "Helvetica",sans-serif; font-size: 10pt;'&gt;As
the work proceeds, crews will be out in force on the rails from Friday night,
November 10, through the early morning hours of Monday, November 13. As a
result, buses will replace trains between Patchogue and Montauk throughout the
weekend. Details are below.&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/p&gt;&lt;font face="Times New Roman" size="3"&gt;

&lt;/font&gt;&lt;p style="background: white; margin: 0in 0in 0pt;"&gt;&lt;em&gt;&lt;u&gt;&lt;span style='font-family: "Helvetica",sans-serif; font-size: 10pt;'&gt;Eastbound&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/u&gt;&lt;/em&gt;&lt;/p&gt;&lt;font face="Times New Roman" size="3"&gt;

&lt;/font&gt;&lt;p style="background: white; margin: 0in 0in 0pt;"&gt;&lt;span style='font-family: "Helvetica",sans-serif; font-size: 10pt;'&gt;Starting with the
8:36 PM eastbound train from Babylon on Friday, November 10, through and
including the 11:26 PM train from Babylon on Sunday, November 12, all eastbound
trains will terminate at Patchogue. Customers going to Bellport,
Mastic-Shirley, Speonk, Westhampton, Hampton Bays, Southampton, Bridgehampton,
East Hampton, Amagansett or Montauk will transfer to buses at Bellport. 
Please plan for 29 minutes of added travel time.&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/p&gt;&lt;font face="Times New Roman" size="3"&gt;

&lt;/font&gt;&lt;p style="background: white; margin: 0in 0in 0pt;"&gt;&lt;span style='font-family: "Calibri",sans-serif; font-size: 11pt;'&gt; &lt;/span&gt;&lt;/p&gt;&lt;font face="Times New Roman" size="3"&gt;

&lt;/font&gt;&lt;p style="background: white; margin: 0in 0in 0pt;"&gt;&lt;span style='font-family: "Calibri",sans-serif; font-size: 11pt;'&gt; &lt;/span&gt;&lt;/p&gt;&lt;font face="Times New Roman" size="3"&gt;

&lt;/font&gt;&lt;p style="background: white; margin: 0in 0in 0pt;"&gt;&lt;em&gt;&lt;u&gt;&lt;span style='font-family: "Helvetica",sans-serif; font-size: 10pt;'&gt;Westbound&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/u&gt;&lt;/em&gt;&lt;/p&gt;&lt;font face="Times New Roman" size="3"&gt;

&lt;/font&gt;&lt;p style="background: white; margin: 0in 0in 0pt;"&gt;&lt;span style='font-family: "Helvetica",sans-serif; font-size: 10pt;'&gt;Starting with the
9:59 PM train from Speonk on Friday, November 10, and continuing through the train
that normally originates at Speonk at 11:56 PM on Sunday, November 12, all
westbound trains will originate at Patchogue at their regularly scheduled
Patchogue departure times. Customers at Montauk, Amagansett, East Hampton,
Bridgehampton, Southampton, Hampton Bays, Westhampton, Speonk, Mastic-Shirley
and Bellport will take buses to Patchogue, then connect with trains for
continuing service to points west. Buses will depart &lt;strong&gt;&lt;span style='font-family: "Helvetica",sans-serif;'&gt;between 16 and 34 minutes earlier &lt;/span&gt;&lt;/strong&gt;than
normal train departure times.&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/p&gt;&lt;font face="Times New Roman" size="3"&gt;
