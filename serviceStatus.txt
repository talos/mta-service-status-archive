&lt;p style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"&gt;&lt;span style="COLOR: black"&gt;&lt;font face="Tahoma"&gt;Buses will replace midday off-peak West Hempstead Branch trains on weekdays from Monday, April 3 through Friday, April 7, while work on the Franklin Avenue grade crossing is underway.  &lt;/font&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"&gt;&lt;span style="COLOR: black"&gt;&lt;font face="Tahoma"&gt; &lt;/font&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"&gt;&lt;span style="COLOR: black"&gt;&lt;font face="Tahoma"&gt;The changes include:&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"&gt;&lt;span style="COLOR: black"&gt;&lt;font face="Tahoma"&gt; &lt;/font&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"&gt;&lt;b&gt;&lt;span style="COLOR: black"&gt;&lt;font face="Tahoma"&gt;Westbound:&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/b&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"&gt;&lt;span style="COLOR: black"&gt;&lt;font face="Tahoma"&gt;Buses will replace the 10:12 AM, 12:12 PM &amp; 2:12 PM trains from West Hempstead. Customers at stations West Hempstead through Westwood will board buses to Valley Stream, then connect with trains for service to pints west.  Buses will depart up to &lt;b&gt;19 minutes later&lt;/b&gt; than normal train times. Please plan for up to 24 minutes of additional travel time.&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"&gt;&lt;b&gt;&lt;span style="COLOR: black"&gt;&lt;font face="Tahoma"&gt; &lt;/font&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/b&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"&gt;&lt;b&gt;&lt;span style="COLOR: black"&gt;&lt;font face="Tahoma"&gt;Eastbound:&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/b&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"&gt;&lt;span style="COLOR: black"&gt;&lt;font face="Tahoma"&gt;Buses will replace the 10:46 AM, 12:46 PM, 2:45 PM and 3:34 PM trains from Valley Stream to West Hempstead. Customers will transfer to buses at Valley Stream for travel to stations Westwood through West Hempstead.  Please plan for up to 18 minutes of additional travel time&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
