&lt;P style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"&gt;&lt;SPAN style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: line"&gt;Buses will replace overnight trains between Huntington and Port Jefferson to accommodate Sperry Rail Testing on Tuesday night, April 17 and Wednesday night, April 18.   Service will be affected through 3:30 AM Thursday morning.   The Sperry Rail Car, a bright yellow vehicle fitted with ultrasonic and induction test equipment, is designed to detect internal rail defects not visible to the naked eye.  The Sperry Rail Car is used to test approximately 500 miles of LIRR track each year.  The inspections are typically performed during off-peak hours to minimize the impact to customers.&lt;/SPAN&gt;&lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"&gt;&lt;SPAN style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: line"&gt; &lt;/SPAN&gt;&lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"&gt;&lt;SPAN style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: line"&gt;Train service will be affected as follows:&lt;/SPAN&gt;&lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"&gt;&lt;SPAN style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: line"&gt; &lt;/SPAN&gt;&lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"&gt;&lt;B&gt;&lt;SPAN style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: line"&gt;Eastbound:&lt;/SPAN&gt;&lt;/B&gt;&lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"&gt;Starting with the connecting train for the 9:42 PM departure from Penn Station on Tuesday night and Wednesday night, four overnight trains to Port Jefferson will terminate at Huntington.&lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt 0.25in; TEXT-INDENT: -0.25in"&gt;&lt;SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: Symbol"&gt;·&lt;/SPAN&gt;&lt;SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 7pt"&gt;         &lt;/SPAN&gt;&lt;SPAN style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: line"&gt;On Tuesday and Wednesday night, the 10:55 PM and 11:49 PM trains from Huntington to Port Jefferson will be replaced by buses. &lt;/SPAN&gt;&lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt 0.25in; TEXT-INDENT: -0.25in"&gt;&lt;SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: Symbol"&gt;·&lt;/SPAN&gt;&lt;SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 7pt"&gt;         &lt;/SPAN&gt;&lt;SPAN style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: line"&gt;On Wednesday and Thursday morning, the 12:55 AM and 2:47 AM trains from Huntington to Port Jefferson will be replaced by buses&lt;/SPAN&gt;&lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"&gt;&lt;SPAN style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: line"&gt; &lt;/SPAN&gt;&lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"&gt;&lt;SPAN style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: line"&gt;Customers for these trains will transfer to buses at Huntington for travel to stations Greenlawn through Port Jefferson. Please plan for up to 40 minutes of additional travel time.&lt;/SPAN&gt;&lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"&gt;&lt;SPAN style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: line"&gt; &lt;/SPAN&gt;&lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"&gt;&lt;B&gt;&lt;SPAN style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: line"&gt;Westbound:&lt;/SPAN&gt;&lt;/B&gt;&lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"&gt;&lt;SPAN style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: line"&gt;The 11:22 PM train from Port Jefferson to Huntington will be replaced by buses. Customers at stations Port Jefferson through Greenlawn will board buses to Huntington, then connect with a train for service to points west.  Buses will depart up to 14 minutes later than normal train times. Please plan for up to 22 minutes of additional travel time&lt;/SPAN&gt;&lt;/P&gt;
