                  &lt;h3&gt;&lt;span&gt;March 31: Buses Replace West Hempstead Branch Trains&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/h3&gt;&lt;h3&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/h3&gt;&lt;p&gt;&lt;span&gt;To accommodate track resurfacing work, buses will replace West Hempstead Branch trains in both directions between Valley Stream and West Hempstead on Saturday, March 31 from 6:50 AM until 2:30 PM.&lt;br&gt;&lt;br&gt;&lt;b&gt;Westbound:&lt;/b&gt;&lt;br&gt;Buses will replace the 6:50 AM, 8:50 AM, 10:50 AM and 12:50 PM trains from West Hempstead. Please plan for additional travel time. &lt;br&gt;&lt;b&gt;&lt;br&gt;Eastbound:&lt;/b&gt;&lt;br&gt;Buses will replace the 8:00 AM, 9:58 AM, 11:58 AM and 1:58 PM trains from Valley Stream to West Hempstead. Customers will transfer to buses at Valley Stream for travel to stations Westwood through West Hempstead. Please plan for &lt;strong&gt;up to 19 minutes of additional travel time&lt;/strong&gt;.&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/p&gt;&lt;p&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;br&gt;Pick up a copy of the special West Hempstead Branch timetable dated March 31, 2018  or see &lt;a href="https://goo.gl/PsG3MAhttp://"&gt;https://goo.gl/PsG3MA&lt;/a&gt;&lt;br&gt;&lt;b&gt;&lt;br&gt;For More Information:&lt;/b&gt;&lt;br&gt;Contact the LIRR's Customer Service Center by calling 511 or 718-217-LIRR (718-217-5477).
