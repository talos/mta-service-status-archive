&lt;P&gt;&lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P&gt;&lt;B&gt;Westbound:&lt;/B&gt;&lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P&gt;·          Customers traveling from stations Ronkonkoma through Pinelawn will board buses to Hicksville, and transfer to train service to points west. &lt;B&gt;Westbound&lt;/B&gt; b&lt;B&gt;uses will depart&lt;/B&gt; &lt;B&gt;up to 44 minutes &lt;U&gt;earlier&lt;/U&gt; than normal train time &lt;/B&gt;in order to connect with trains in Hicksville.&lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P&gt;&lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P&gt;·          The10:05 AM, 11:05 AM and the 12:05 PM westbound trains from Ronkonkoma will originate instead at Farmingdale. Bus service will NOT be provided from Ronkonkoma to Hicksville for these three trains.&lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P&gt;&lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P&gt; &lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P&gt;&lt;B&gt;Eastbound:&lt;/B&gt;&lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P&gt;·          Customers traveling to stations Pinelawn through Ronkonkoma will board buses at Hicksville. Please plan for up to 44 minutes of extra travel time.&lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P&gt;&lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P&gt;·          The4:32 PM, 5:32 PM and 6:32 PM eastbound trains from Penn Station will terminate at Farmingdale. Bus service will NOT be provided from Hicksville to Ronkonkoma for these three trains.&lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P&gt; &lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P&gt;&lt;B&gt;East of Ronkonkoma (North Fork) Customers:&lt;/B&gt;&lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P&gt;Weekend Greenport service is also affected on both weekends (August 13 &amp; 14 and 20 &amp; 21) with adjusted schedules.  &lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P&gt; &lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P&gt;&lt;B&gt;Westbound:&lt;/B&gt;&lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P&gt;Westbound customers from North Fork stations Greenport through Medford board regularly scheduled trains and transfer to buses at Ronkonkoma, 21 minutes later for Hicksville, where train service to points west will resume. Westbound North Fork customers will arrive at LIRR western terminals one hour later than normal. Westbound North Fork customers traveling to stations Central Islip through Bethpage will board a local bus at Ronkonkoma to their final destinations. &lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P&gt; &lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P&gt;&lt;B&gt;Eastbound:&lt;/B&gt;&lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P&gt;·          Eastbound customers, who normally take the 9:12 AM from Penn Station to Ronkonkoma to connect with the train to Greenport, must &lt;B&gt;instead take a train one hour earlier than normal, departing Penn Station at 8:12 AM&lt;/B&gt; for Hicksville. Then, at Hicksville transfer to buses to Ronkonkoma where normal train service to Greenport will resume. Customers at stations Bethpage through Central Islip will board a local bus for Ronkonkoma, where normal train service to Greenport will resume.&lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P&gt;·          Eastbound customers, who normally take the 2:12 PM from Penn Station to Ronkonkoma to connect with the train to Greenport, must &lt;B&gt;instead take a train one hour earlier than normal, departing Penn Station at 1:12 PM&lt;/B&gt; for Hicksville.Then, at Hicksville transfer to buses to Ronkonkoma where normal train service to Greenport will resume. Customers at stations Bethpage through Central Islip will board a local bus for Ronkonkoma, where normal train service to Greenport will resume.&lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P&gt;  &lt;BR&gt;&lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P&gt;&lt;B&gt;To avoid busing on the weekend of August 13-14 and August 20-21, customers may wish to use the Babylon, Montauk or Port Jefferson Branches, as these branches will not be affected by trackwork.&lt;/B&gt;&lt;/P&gt;
