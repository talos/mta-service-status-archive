&lt;p class="MsoNoSpacing" style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"&gt;From Atlantic Terminal: Board a Manhattan-bound D, N or Q subway train and ride to 34 St-Herald Square for a one-block walk to Penn Station to connect with eastbound LIRR service.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p class="MsoNoSpacing" style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"&gt;From Nostrand Avenue:  Board a Manhattan-bound A subway train at Fulton St. &amp; Nostrand Av., two blocks from the LIRR station, and ride to 34 St-Penn Station to connect with eastbound LIRR service.  &lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p class="MsoNoSpacing" style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"&gt;From East New York:  Board a Manhattan-bound L subway train to Broadway Junction, then transfer to a Queens-bound J subway train and ride to Sutphin Blvd-Archer Av to connect with eastbound LIRR service from Jamaica.&lt;/p&gt;

&lt;p class="MsoNoSpacing" style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"&gt;To Nostrand Avenue:  Take an LIRR train to Penn Station, then board a Brooklyn-bound A subway train for travel to Nostrand Av.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p class="MsoNoSpacing" style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"&gt;To East New York:  Take an LIRR train to Jamaica, then board a Brooklyn-bound J subway train to Broadway Junction to transfer to a Rockaway Parkway-bound L subway to East New York.&lt;/p&gt;
