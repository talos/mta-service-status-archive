&lt;P&gt;&lt;B&gt;Port Jefferson Branch Weekend Trains Affected by Rail &amp; Switch Replacement August 6-7&lt;/B&gt;&lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P&gt;&lt;I&gt;Work results in service reduction and Busing Between Hicksville &amp; Huntington Sunday Evening&lt;/I&gt;&lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P&gt;This weekend, train service on the Port Jefferson Branch will be affected by rail and switch replacement work in the Huntington vicinity. On the weekend of August 6 and 7, one track will be taken out of service between Syosset and Huntington for 48 hours. As a result:&lt;/P&gt;
&lt;UL style="MARGIN-TOP: 0in" type=disc&gt;
&lt;LI&gt;Half hourly service will be reduced to hourly service between Huntington and Hicksville&lt;/LI&gt;
&lt;LI&gt;90-minute service to/from Port Jefferson will be reduced to 2-hourly intervals &lt;/LI&gt;&lt;/UL&gt;
&lt;P&gt;Additionally, beginning 4 PM Sunday, August 7 and continuing through 1 AM Monday, August 8, buses will replace all trains between Hicksville and Huntington.  &lt;/P&gt;
&lt;UL style="MARGIN-TOP: 0in" type=disc&gt;
&lt;LI&gt;Eastbound customers traveling to stations Syosset through Huntington will detrain at Hicksville for buses to their final destinations. Train service to Port Jefferson will resume at Huntington. Please plan for up to 30 minutes additional travel time.  &lt;/LI&gt;
&lt;LI&gt;Westbound customers at stations Huntington through Syosset will board buses &lt;U&gt;up to 35 minutes earlier than normal train times &lt;/U&gt;and transfer to trains at Hicksville for service to points west.  Westbound customers traveling from stations Port Jefferson through Greenlawn will detrain at Huntington and board buses for Hicksville for train connections to western terminals. &lt;/LI&gt;&lt;/UL&gt;Please plan for up to 30minutes of additional travel time
