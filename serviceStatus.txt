                  &lt;h3&gt;&lt;span&gt;Monday, April 30 - Weekday Schedule Changes Begin on Ronkonkoma Branch&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/h3&gt;&lt;h3&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/h3&gt;&lt;span&gt;As the Double Track project moves into its final phases, weekday schedule changes are needed in order to remove the existing siding at Central Islip and install the second through-running track. Although most changes impact off-peak service, several rush hour trains are affected, starting Monday, April 30 and continuing through the end of summer.  Changes include:&lt;br&gt;&lt;br&gt;&lt;u&gt;&lt;i&gt;Westbound:&lt;/i&gt;&lt;/u&gt;&lt;br&gt; ·       &lt;b&gt;The 7:04AM peak train from Ronkonkoma to Penn Station will originate at Brentwood. The train will depart Brentwood at 7:15 AM, with added stops at Jamaica and Kew Gardens, arriving Penn Station at 8:23 AM.  Customers who normally board this train at Ronkonkoma can take alternate trains departing 8 minutes earlier at 6:56 AM or 15 minutes later at 7:19 AM.&lt;br&gt; &lt;/b&gt;·       The 4:11 PM train from Ronkonkoma will depart two minutes earlier at all stops through Bethpage (Ronkonkoma 4:09; Central Islip 4:16; Brentwood 4:20; Deer Park 4:25; Wyandanch 4:30; Farmingdale 4:36; Bethpage 4:41 PM), arriving Penn Station at5:38 PM. &lt;br&gt;·       The 7:13 PM and 7:58 PM trains from Ronkonkoma will be combined into a new train, departing Ronkonkoma at 7:45 PM (stopping Central Islip 7:52; Brentwood 7:59; Deer Park 8:04; Wyandanch 8:09; Farmingdale 8:15; Bethpage 8:20; Hicksville 8:26; Mineola 8:36 PM), arriving Penn Station at 9:12 PM. &lt;br&gt;&lt;br&gt;Eastbound:&lt;br&gt; ·       The 5:11 AM train from Penn Station to Ronkonkoma will terminate at Brentwood at 6:22 AM. Substitute buses will be provided for customers traveling to Central Islip and Ronkonkoma. Please plan for 31 minutes extra travel time. &lt;br&gt;·       The 3:07 PM train from Penn Station to Ronkonkoma (usual arrival time 4:25 PM) will depart Penn two minutes later at 3:09 PM and arrive Ronkonkoma at 4:30 PM. &lt;br&gt;·       The 7:11 PM peak train from Penn Station to Ronkonkoma (usual arrival time 8:33 PM) will depart Penn six minutes later at 7:17 PM and arrive Ronkonkoma at 8:43 PM. &lt;br&gt;&lt;br&gt;&lt;b&gt;Pickup a copy of the Ronkonkoma Branch weekday timetable dated April 30, 2018 or link here: &lt;/b&gt; &lt;a href="http://web.mta.info/lirr/Timetable/4-30%20to%205-18-2018%20-%20KO%20-%20ML2T%20Cutovers%20-%20GO%20304.pdf"&gt;RonkonkomaBranch&lt;/a&gt;&lt;br&gt;&lt;br&gt;&lt;b&gt;For more information&lt;/b&gt;, contact the LIRR's Customer Service Center by calling 511 or 718-217-LIRR (718-217-5477).
