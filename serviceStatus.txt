&lt;P&gt;&lt;B&gt;April 29-30: Weekend Babylon Branch Service Reduced &lt;/B&gt;&lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P&gt;&lt;B&gt; &lt;/B&gt;&lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P&gt;To accommodate concrete tie installation work, one of two tracks between Valley Stream and Freeport will be taken out of service starting at 12:35 AM on Saturday, April 29 through 3:30 AM on Monday, May 1.  As a result, weekend train service on the Babylon Branch will be reduced from half-hourly to hourly during certain hours. Train service will be affected as follows:. &lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P&gt; &lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P&gt;&lt;B&gt;Eastbound&lt;/B&gt;&lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P&gt;Trains that depart Penn Station at 6:30 AM, 8:30 AM, 9:30AM, 10:30 AM, 11:30 AM, 12:30 PM, 1:30 PM, 2:30 PM, 5:30 PM, 6:30 PM, 7:30 PM,9:30 PM, 10:30 PM and 11:30 PM will terminate at Jamaica. Eastbound customers traveling to stations Rockville Centre through Babylon will have alternate train service 30 minutes earlier or 30 minutes later. &lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P&gt; &lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P&gt;&lt;B&gt;Westbound&lt;/B&gt;&lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P&gt;Trains that depart Babylon at 8:23 AM, 9:23 AM, 10:23 AM,11:23 AM, 12:23 PM, 2:23 PM, 5:23 PM, 6:23 PM, 7:23 PM, 9:23 PM and 10:24 PM will be canceled. Customers at stations Babylon through Rockville Centre will have alternate train service 30 minutes earlier or 30 minutes later.&lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P&gt; &lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P&gt;To see the special Babylon Branch weekend timetable dated April 29-30, link here: &lt;A href="http://web.mta.info/supplemental/lirr/weekendbabylonbranchservicereduced517.htm"&gt;http://web.mta.info/supplemental/lirr/weekendbabylonbranchservicereduced517.htm&lt;/A&gt;&lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P&gt; &lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P&gt;&lt;B&gt;Please plan ahead:&lt;/B&gt; Similar service changes will also be in effect for May 6-7, Saturday, May 13 and May 20-21, 2017. Normal weekend service is anticipated for Mother's Day, Sunday, May 14.&lt;/P&gt;
