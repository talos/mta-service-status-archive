&lt;p&gt;&lt;b&gt;Tuesday, December 12: Buses Replace Trains between Ronkonkoma &amp; Greenport&lt;/b&gt;&lt;/p&gt;

&lt;p&gt;Buses replace eight  off-peak and PM Peak trains east of Ronkonkoma starting at 7:30 AM on Tuesday, December 12 to accommodate switch replacement work at Mill Road in Riverhead. Service will be affected as follows:&lt;/p&gt;

&lt;p&gt;&lt;b&gt;Eastbound:&lt;/b&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;The 7:30 AM, 12:42 PM, 6:53 PM trains from Ronkonkoma to Greenport and the 5:21 PM train from Ronkonkoma to Yaphank are cancelled. Customers will board buses at Ronkonkoma for transportation to stations Medford to Greenport.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li&gt;Please plan for up to 18 minutes of additional travel time.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt;

&lt;p&gt;&lt;b&gt;Westbound:&lt;/b&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;The 9:43 AM, 2:43 PM, 9:39 PM trains from Greenport to Ronkonkoma and the 6:00 PM train from Yaphank to Ronkonkoma are cancelled.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li&gt;Customers at stations Greenport through Medford will board buses to Ronkonkoma.
