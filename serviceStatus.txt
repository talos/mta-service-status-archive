&lt;P style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"&gt;&lt;SPAN style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: line"&gt;&lt;STRONG&gt;4/17-19:  Buses Replace Overnight Trains between Huntington and Port Jefferson&lt;/STRONG&gt;&lt;/SPAN&gt;&lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"&gt;&lt;SPAN style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: line"&gt;&lt;/SPAN&gt; &lt;/P&gt;
&lt;P style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"&gt;&lt;SPAN style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: line"&gt;Buses will replace overnight trains between Huntington and Port Jefferson to accommodate Sperry Rail Testing on Tuesday night, April 17 and Wednesday night, April 18.   Service will be affected through 3:30 AM Thursday morning.   The Sperry Rail Car, a bright yellow vehicle fitted with ultrasonic and induction test equipment, is designed to detect internal rail defects not visible to the naked eye.  The Sperry Rail Car is used to test approximately 500 miles of LIRR track each year.  The inspections are typically performed during off-peak hours to minimize the impact to customers.&lt;/SPAN&gt;&lt;/P&gt;
