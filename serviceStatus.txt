will be reduced from half-hourly to hourly during certain hours.  Train
service will be affected as follows:&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/p&gt;&lt;font face="Times New Roman" size="3"&gt;

9:30 PM, 10:30 PM and 11:30 PM will terminate at Jamaica.  Eastbound customers

train service 30 minutes earlier or 30 minutes later. &lt;/font&gt;&lt;/p&gt;&lt;font face="Times New Roman" size="3"&gt;
